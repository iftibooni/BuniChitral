Garam Chashma, a scenic valley in Lower Chitral, is famed for its natural hot springs and serene mountain setting. Surrounded by rugged peaks and lush greenery, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. The mineral-rich waters are believed to have healing properties, attracting visitors year-round. The valley is also home to a vibrant culture and traditional lifestyle. With its peaceful atmosphere, flowing streams, and welcoming locals, Garam Chashma is an ideal retreat for those seeking comfort, nature, and cultural charm.
