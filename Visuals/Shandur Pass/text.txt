Shandur Pass, known as the “Roof of the World,” is a breathtaking high-altitude plateau connecting Chitral with Gilgit. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and vast open landscapes, it sits at over 3,700 meters above sea level. The pass is famous for hosting the annual Shandur Polo Festival, where traditional freestyle polo is played with unmatched passion. With its serene lake, cool mountain air, and dramatic scenery, Shandur offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty for travelers.
