❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel any booking from your bookings list. Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy (100% refund if cancelled 7+ days before, 50% for 3-7 days, no refund within 3 days).

How do payments work?

We accept JazzCash (for Pakistan) and Stripe (international). You'll receive payment instructions after booking confirmation.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes! Contact the guide through the booking details to discuss modifications. Changes may affect pricing.

What if the guide cancels?

You'll receive a full refund immediately and will be offered alternative guides.
